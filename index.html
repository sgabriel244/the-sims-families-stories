The Sims Families Stories

The Sims is a successful gaming franchise. Its first release was back in February 4th, 2000. After 4 generations, it remains a beloved series among gamers around the world.

Despite its success, The Sims has a gameplay lore of its worlds that is not well known to the public. This website is dedicated to sharing the stories of the families that have lived in the world of The Sims, and how they have evolved over time.
